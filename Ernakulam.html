There are plethora of Kochi tourist places that attract a large number of visitors every year mainly because of its unspoilt natural beauty. Fort Kochi, Willingdon Island, Bolgatty Island, Wonderla Amusement park, a Folklore museum and the Kerala Kathakali centre are a few of the prime tourist destinations in Kochi.

Kochi also offers the best Seafood cuisine cooked the traditional way of doing justice to the fresh catches of the day.

Major attractions

Jew Synagogue and jewish streat

The Jewish Synagogue is the oldest active Synagogue in the Commonwealth of Nations making it one of the hotspots amongst Kochi. A narrow street between the Synagogue and Mattancherry Palace is Jew town, a den for antique lovers. Little fractions of history crafted in metal and wood waiting to be owned by a curious tourist.

Visit
Andhakaranazhi beach

This beach drawing several tourists is located in a coastal village, located 4km of Pattanakkad. The tall lighthouse in white and red grabs attention as it helps navigate the seamen at night.

Visit
Kumbalangi villege

Enclosed by backwaters, this village has a ring of Chinese fishing nets and local paddy cultivation. This place offers a beautiful glimpse of the wealthy livelihood of the villagers.
