Major attractions

Muzhapilangad beach

Famous as Kerala’s only drive-in beach, the Muzhappilangad offers some of the most stunning views of the sun setting along the golden sandy beaches of Malabar coast. The 4 km stretch of land running along the ocean is perfect for taking a romantic, leisurely stroll or just drive on the beach and soak in the naked natural splendour around.

Visit
Kannur light house

The vantage point offers spectacular and awe-inspiring views of the entire district of Kannur, the vast expanse of the Arabian sea, and the Baby beach. The bewitching backdrop delights the viewer and the feeling the wind on your face at the highest point will leave you truly breathless.

Visit
St Angelo fort

The fort is triangular in shape appears as a massive yet magnificent structure standing at the helm of the ruthless sea. The laterite walls made of stone lend it an even more imposing air and it is a must-visit destination for architecture students. The sea wall segregating the fort from the sea provides breathtaking views of the Mopilla Bay.
